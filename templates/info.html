How to play

Guess the phrase

The phrase is given with missing words.

Figure out the missing words based on their shape!

Ascending letters: "b", "d", "f", "h", "i", "k", "l", "t", will be displayed as "█".

Descending letters: "g", "j", "p", "q", "y", will be displayed as "_".

All other letters are nuetral and will be displayed as "▄".

Example:

██'▄ the ▄█▄_▄ of ▄ circle

it's the shape of a circle
